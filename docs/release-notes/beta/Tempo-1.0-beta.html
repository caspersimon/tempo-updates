Tempo Beta 1.0

Build 2

This beta refresh focuses on making Tempo feel more reliable and more polished in day-to-day use.

What's improved

  • Fixed a first-run issue where transcription could fail immediately after onboarding in fresh installs.
  • Improved onboarding for model downloads, including queueing and progress behavior.
  • Better handling of existing local model caches and sandboxed AI cleanup flows.
  • Fixed a queue deletion crash.
  • Refined window behavior and visual consistency across the app, including About, Settings, and Open Source Licences.

This is still a beta build, but it should feel noticeably closer to the intended day-to-day experience than the previous beta.
